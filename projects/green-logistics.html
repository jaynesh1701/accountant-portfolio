Strategic financial planning and tax structuring for a major entertainment production.

Client

Kasumbo Productions

Service

Tax Structuring

Year

2023

Outcome

15% Tax Efficiency

The Challenge

Managing the finances of a large-scale film production involves complex regulatory compliance, multiple vendor payments, and strict budget adherence. The client needed a robust system to track daily expenses and ensure tax compliance across different jurisdictions.

Our Approach

We implemented a real-time expense tracking system and structured the entity to optimize GST input credits. By centralizing the financial workflow, we ensured that every rupee was accounted for and that the production stayed compliant with local tax laws.

The Result

The production was completed on budget, with zero compliance issues. Our strategic tax planning saved the client approximately 15% in overall tax liabilities, allowing them to reinvest in marketing and distribution.
